Our Mission

Built on Real Clinic Needs

We've interviewed 30+ federally qualified health centers and safety-net clinics to understand the real challenges they face with technology adoption. The problem is clear: clinics need AI and clinical technology to reduce administrative burden, but they lack the time, resources, and bandwidth to experiment with new tools.

clinicAIre exists to bridge this gap — connecting under-resourced clinics with vetted technology companies through managed pilots and comprehensive implementation support.

Our Approach

The Matching Algorithm

Intelligent matching that minimizes risk and maximizes value for every clinic.

Tier 1: Deal-Breakers

Hard constraints like budget caps, EHR compatibility, regulatory requirements, and PHI compliance. If any fail, the match is rejected immediately.

Tier 2: Fit & Value

Assesses problem-solution alignment, workflow integration, implementation burden, and expected ROI. Weighted scoring based on clinic priorities.

Tier 3: Optimization

Fine-tunes matches based on support needs, training requirements, change management complexity, and vendor track record in similar settings.

Our algorithm continuously learns from pilot outcomes, improving recommendations with every implementation.
